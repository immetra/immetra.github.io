Featured Works

These are some of the projects that we have delivered.

Complaint and Claims System

A web system that allows customer to submit complaints that will be processed by support personnel, leading up to claims approval and payment.

Distribution Box Monitoring

A front-end for an OPC/SCADA that shows graphical status of some electrical distribution (DB) boxes spread across the nation. The application interacts with an OPC server to acquire the status, gather reports, and show alerts. and also able to initiate actions such as resetting a circuit breaker switch.

Courier Sales and Billing System

Sales and billing system for a courier business to manage it's contract customers and their billings. This includes the registration process for contract customers, products and pricing, rebates and discounts, disputes management, and invoice generation.

Communication Line Status Monitoring

A front-end for an OPC/SCADA setup that shows graphical status of communication devices of a military agency. The application interacts with an OPC server to acquire the status, gather reports, and alerts.

Feedback Management System

Starting with a feedback form from the corporate website, a suggestion, complaint, or enquiry is then routed to appropriate departments for action. A designated customer service representative then replies with a proper follow-up.

Question Bank System

A system to compose and collect questions for exams. Question will go through various QA to ensure its quality. Exam papers can then be generated on the fly based on pre-define templates.

Key Performance Indicator Tracking System

A system to register and assign KPI items and areas for employee. Outcomes can then be rated and assessed. Integrated with SAP Human Resource module.

Investigation Record System

A system to register reports of misuse and tampering of company assets which SITREP and further investigations can be done. SLA is put is place to ensure responsiveness.

Capital Expenditure Budget System

Management and approval of budget allocation, requests and returns from division to departments and projects. Integrated with SAP Investment Management module.

Fibre Optic Leasing System

A system to manage leasing request, commisioning, terminations, and billing of fibre optic infrastructure and services. Intergrates with third party Asset Management System and SAP Billing module.

Idea Bank System

Provides methods for submission of ideas which then can be peer-evaluated, endorsed, and implemented. Outcome then can tracked and monitored using reports and scorecards.

Project Progress Dashboard

An executive dashboard and statistics for projects management. Integrated with Microsoft Enterprise Project Management Server.

Vendor Registration Portal

Provides means for suppliers and contractors to submit their application to be vendors for an organization. Modules include registration, evaluation, performance tracking, training, event and surveys.

Product Certification System

Allows manufacturer to acquire certification for their products from a government agency. Includes assessement, surveillance, quotation generation, and QR code distribution modules.

* Some projects are kept discreet in their description as requested by our partners and clients.
